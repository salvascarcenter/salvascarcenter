== Description ==
Car Fix Lite is a visually refreshing, youthful and vibrant, intuitive and modern, purposeful and deliberate, extensively designed and carefully crafted, clean and clutter-free, car repair WordPress theme. It is a reliable set of tools for developing effective and professional websites for your automobile business. This theme especially designed to completely satisfying the needs of wide range of auto service applications, ranging from car repair shops, car washes, mechanics shops, brakes shops, wheel shop, car dealers, garages, car rental agencies and many other small auto business services.
